**2 Nagaray alame gelides**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 1. Addaameynne iza machcha GODAY isttas tokkida atakilte giddon de7iza wode keehi ufayettishe deettes. Nam7ayka kallo deettes; gido attiin banta kalloteththan issoy issaas yeellatibeetenna; gaasoyka he wode nagaray alame bolla deenna. Istti atakilteza giddon simerettishe GODAARA haasayettishe deettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Gido attiin genanchcha shooshshay atakilte giddon dees. Izi maccassayo, <<GODAY inttena tumukka atakilte giddon diza miththa ayfeppe awayssafekka monta mala azazidee?>> gi oychchides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3. Maccassayakka, <<GODAY iitanne lo7o erisiza erateththa miththa ayfey attoriin ubba miththa ayfekka nu maanaas dandayizayssa yootides. Gido attiin he miththaa ayfe intte miikko woykko intte bochchiko intte hayqana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 4. Shooshshazi maccassays, <<Hayssi tumu gidenna; intte hayqeketa: intte ayfezappe miza wode intte ayfey doyetanayssa, iitanne kiya eron intte Xoossa mala gidanayssa GODAY eriza gish inttena mopite giday hessassa>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 5. Maccassaya miththaa ayfezi meeththas lo7o, ayfes ufayssizazinne erateth demmanaas yaarechchizaz gididayssa be7ida wode ayfezappe ekka madus. Banara diza ba azinaska immadus; izikka miththaa ayfeza mides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 6. Heerakka nam7aa ayfetikka doyettida. Nam7ayka banti kallo dizayssa akeekidi banta kallo kammanaas boncco may7o mala sikkida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 7.Addezinne maccassaya Xoossa cengursi atakilte giddon simerettishin siydi nam7ayka Xoossa sinththafe baqatida (qotettida). Xoossi Addaame xeygidi, <<Awan de7ay? >> gi oychchides. Addaamey, <<Neni atakilteson simerettishin siyada kallo diza gishshas babbada qotetadis>> gi zaarides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 8. GODAYKKA, <<Ne kallo dizayssa oone nees yootiday? Ta nena, <Mooppa> gida miththay ayfeppe madii?>> gides. Addamikka, <<Hanna ne tanara daana mala immida maccassaya miththaa ayfezappe taas immin madis>> gides. GODAY Maccassayo, <<Hayssi ne ooththidayssi aazee?>> gides. Izakka, <<Tana shooshshi baleththiin madis>> gadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg?nolink&}}

2.9. GODAY shooshshaa, <<Neni qangetidayssa; neni tiran gooshshetana; neni biitta maana; neninne maccasiya intte giddon morke gidana; ne naytinne izi naytikka banta giddon morke gidana; maccassay zarey nees hu7e qoxxana; nenikka izas qirphe madunththana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 10. GODAY maccassayo, <<Miixa wode ta nees ceece darssana; ne sheney ne azinakko gidana; izikka ne bolla godatana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 11. GODAY Addaames, <<Ne machchey qaala siyada ta nena mooppa gida miththay ayfeppe ne mida gish biittay neeppe dendidayssan qangetadus. Ne de7o layth ubbaan ne maana kath un7etta ooththada izippe demmana. Hessafe guye ne biitta gidida gish hayqqada biittan simmana>> gides. Addami ba machcho iza shempora dizayta ubbaa aayo gidida gish <<Heewano>> gi sunththides; izas birshshechay, <<Shemppo immizaaro>> guussa. GODAY mehe galba giigissidi Addaamenne Heewano mayzides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?nolink&}}

  - GODAY, <<Asi lo7onne iita shaakki eranaas ha7i nuuppe issaa gidides; qasseka zaari ba kushe yeddidi de7o miththa ayfe ekki moonta malanne merinaas de7onta mala>> giidi Addaamenne Heewano lo7iza atakilte giddofe kessides. Hessafe guye de7o miththaykko efiza oge naagana malanne oonikka he de7o miththay ayfe ekki moonta mala wolqama kiitanchcha atakilteson woththides.

**3. Bashsha haath**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 1. Daro wodeppe guye alame bolla daro derey duussi doommides. Isttikka keehi iitatanne wolqqamata gidida. Iitateththi keehi dari dari yida gish GODAY alame ubbaa bashsha haaththan dhayssana gishe qachchides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Gido attiin Nohey GODAA matan teemateth demmides. Nohey ba de7iza layththan diza asaappe bollara geeshshanne mooroppe haakkida asa. GODAY ba yeddana bashsha haaththa gishshas izas yootides. Nohey gita markabe ooththana mala GODAY izas yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3. GODAYKKA Nohey markabe ooththana mala yootishe, <<Markabeys adussateththi 140 metire, aahoteththi 23 metire, dhoqqateththi 13.5 metire gido>> gides. Nohey ooththida markabeya miththafe oosetadus; izis heedzdzu fooqey, daro kifleti, kaaraynne maskootey deettes. Markabeyakka Nohenne izaso asaa, biitta bolla diza ubba qommo medoosata bashsha haaththafe naagana malas yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 4 Nohey GODAAS azazettidi izi yootida mala Noheynne heedzdzu naa izayti markabeyo ooththida. Markabeya keehi gita gidida gish ooththishin daro layth ekkadus. Nohey yaana bashsha haaththa gishshas deraa minththi seerishe istti GODAAKO simmana mala yootides; gidikkoka istti izi gizayssa ammanibeetenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 5 GODAY Nohenne izaso asaa bantasinne medoosatas gidana likke kath dagassana mala azazides. Ubbaa miishshi giigidaappe guye markabeyin gelana wodey gakkidayssa Nohes, iza machcheys, iza naytasinne nayta machchetas yootides. Osppun asati markabeyin gelida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 6. GODAY bashsha haaththafe naagettana mala ubbatappe maccanne adde histtidi medoosatanne kafota Nohekko yeddides. Yarshshos go7iza laappun addenne laappun macca ubba qommo medoosatappe Nohe markabekko yeddides. Ubbaykka markabe giddo gelida mala GODAY barka markabeza penge gordides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 7. Hessafe guye iray buko dommides. Bukkishe bukkishe bukkishe aggonta 40 gallasinne omars kumeth bukkides. Biittafe pulttoy pude pultees; kumeththa alame bolla diza miishshi ubbay haaththan kamettides; gita zumatikka attonta haaththan kamettida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 8. Markabeza giddon diza asatappenne medosatappe attoorin biitta bolla de7iza ubbay hayqqides. Markabeyaka haaththaa bolla pude dendada ba giddon diza shemppota ubbaa hayqoppe ashshadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 9. Irazi bukkizayssa aggidaappe guye markabeya ichchash agina likke haaththa bolla dendadus. Hessafe guye haaththazi pacci pacci bides; gallassatappe issi gallas markabeya zumata xeeran shempadus. Gido attiin alamey hanno gakkanaas haaththan kametta uttadus. Heedzdzu aginappe guyen zumata xeeray beeto doommides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 10. Nohey oyddu tammu gallassafe guye biitta bollafe haaththazi melidaakko eranaas quuro yeddides. Quurozi haanne heenne piradhishe mela biitta koydi demmanaas dandayibeenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 11. Hessafe guye Nohey haraphphe yeddides. Iza gidikko ba uttanaso demmanaas dandyonta gish simmada Nohekko yadus. Saamintafe guye Nohey zaaridi haraphpheyo yeddides. Izakka wogara miththa boncco ba doonan ekkada simmadus. Haaththay pacci pacci biza gish doozatikka dolo doommida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 12.Nohey hara issi saaminta naagidaappe guye haraphpheyo heedzdzanththo yuusho yeddides. He wode ba shempana soho demmida gish simmada yabeeku. Haaththaayka meli meli bees.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 13. Nam7u aginappe guye GODAY Nohe, <<Neni, neso asatinne medoosati ubbay markabey giddofe kezite>> gides. Izaso asay markabey giddofe kezida. Daro naytanne naa nayta yelidi biitta kumida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 14. Nohey markabeyppe ke7idaappe guye yarshshoso ooththidi ubba medoosatappe yarshshos shiiqanaas bessizayta GODAAS yarshsho shiishshides. GODAY yarshshozan ufayettidi Nohenne izaso asaa anjjides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 15. GODAY, <<Asi ooththiza iita miishsha gish zaarada tani biitta mulekka doqqonta mala qaala gelays. Aykkoska asa nayti yelagateththafe doommidi nagaranchchata gidikkoka alameza ta zaarada bashsha haaththan dhayssike>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg?nolink&}}

  - Caaqo qaalas malata gidana mala GODAY koyro zuulla ooththides. Ubba wode zuullazi salo bolla beettees. GODAY ha zuulla baggara deraas gelida qaala qoppees.

**4. GODAA caaqo qaala Abrhaamera;**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 1. Bashsha haaththafe haa simmiin daro layththafe guye derey alame bolla zaari tirpides. Ubbayka issi qaalan haasayeettes. GODAY istta azazida mala biitta kumanayssa aggidi issi bolla shiiqettidi katama keexxida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Isttikka otoranchchatanne Xoossi isttas yootidayssa naagettontayta. Ubbarakka salo gakkanaas aduussa gimbe gimbeth doommida. GODAY hayti iita ooththizayssanne hessan gujji gujji bizayssa be7ides. Hessafekka keehi iita nagara ooththanaas dandayeettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3.Hessa gishshas GODAY istta qaala daro dumma dumma qaalan laammidi asaa biitta bolla laallides. Istti ooththanaas doommida katamay sunththi Babiloone geetettawus. Izis sunththa birshshechay bul7akettidaaz woykko hadirdaaz guussa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 4. Xeetu layththafe guyen GODAY Abraame geetettiza bitanezas haasaydes. GODAY iza, <<Ne derenne neso asaa aggada ta nena bessiza biitta ba. Heen ta nena anjjana; bayra dere kessana; ne sunththaa gitasana; nena anjjizayta ta anjjana; nena doqqizayta tani doqqana; ne gaason biitta bolla diza as ubbay anjjetana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 5. Abraamey GODAAS azazettides; ba machcho Soora, ba ashkara ubbatanne baas diza aqota ubbaa ekkidi Xoossi iza bessida Kanaane biitta bides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 6. Abraami Kanaane gakkida wode GODAY, <<Ne yuushon dizayssa ubbaa xeella; ne be7iza biitta ubbaa ta nessinne ne zareththatas laata histta immana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 7. Gallassatappe issi gallas Abraami Melke-Xedeqe geetettiza gita Xoossa qeesetas halaqaara gayttides. Melke-Xedeqey Abraame anjjishe, <<Salaanne sa7a Goday gita Xoossay Abraame anjjo>> gides. Abraami baas diza ubbaafe Melke-Xedeqes asraata immides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 8. Abraameynne Sooray daro layththafe guyenka nay baynda deettes. GODAY Abraames zaaridi hidota qaala immides; izas nay daanayssanne iza zarey salo xoolinte mala daranayssa yootides. Abraami GODAY immida hidota qaalan ammanides. Abraami GODAY immida hidota qaalan ammanida gishshas geesh gidi qoodettides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 9. GODAY Abraamera caaqo qaala gelides. Caaqo qaalay nam7u bagga asata giddon diza sigeteththa yo7o. GODAYKKA Abraames, <<Ta nees ne gulbateppe naa immana; ne zaretaska Kanaane biitta immana>> gides. He wode Abraames nay deenna.

  - **Hidota immetida naaza**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Hessa gish Abraami Aggaaro ekkides. Aggaara atuma naa yeladus. Abraami, <<Isma7eele>> gishe izas sunth kessides. Gido attiin Sooray Aggaari bolla qanaatadus; Isma7eeles tammanne heedzdzu layth gidishin,

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3. GODAY Abraame, <<Tani ubbaa dandayza Xoossa; nenara tani qaala gelana>> gides. Abraamey liiphe bolla sinththa guth gufannides; GODAYKKA Abraame, <<Neni daro deres aawa gidana. Kanaane biitta neessinne ne zaretas xinxo histta immana. Tanikka merinaas intes Xoossaa gidana; nenikka neson de7iza addeta ubbaa qaxxara>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 4. GODAY, <<Ne machcha Sooray attuma naa yelana; izikka hidota immetida naaza gidana; iza sunththaa ne Yisaaqa ga xeygana; ta caaqo qaala izara woththana; izikka bayra dere gidana. Isma7eelekka gita dere histtana; gido attiin ta caaqo qaalay Yisaaqara gidana>> gides. Xoossi Abraame sunththa, <<Abrahaame>> gi laammides; izas birshshechay, <<Darota aawaa>> guussa. **30. Yesusi ichchashu shi asa mizides**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Yesusi daro dumma dumma gutatan bidi sabbakana malanne tamarsana mala Hawareta kiitides. Istti Yesusi dizaso simmidappe guye bantti ooththidayssa izas yootida. Hessafe guye Yesusi abbafe findi wocamay baynda woppaso issife biidi heen shempana mala yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Gido attiin wogolon Yesusinne iza tamaareti bishin daro asati be7ida. Ha asati isttafe sinththatanas abba matara woxida. Hessa gishshas Yesusinne iza tamaaretibabba pini kanththiza wode keha daro asati isttafe sinththati pindi heen istta nagetes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Asay maccassatinne nayti qodetonta addeti xalla 5000 bolla. Yesusi deras keha qadhettides.Yesusas ha asati henththanchchay baynda dorsata mala. Hessa gishshas istta tamarsidi istta giddon diza harganchchata paththides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg?nolink&}}

He gallassa omarsi iza tamaareti; <<Gadey qamida gishshas heeran katamati deettenna. Istti banttas maana miishshi shaman mala deraa moyza>> gi yootida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Gido attiin Yesusi ba tamaareta; <<Istti maana miishshi intte isttas immite!>> gides. Isttika; <<Hayssaththo oosoy waani dandayetanee? Nuus dizay ichashshu soolonne nam7u mole xalla>> gidi zaarida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Ichashshu tamu ichashshu tamu gididi mata bolla uttana mala asas yootana mala Yesusi ba tamaaretas yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye Yesusiichashshu soollonne nam7u mole oykidi salo xeelides. Kaththa gishshas Xoossaa galatides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Histtidi Yesusi soollozanne moleza shaakides. Asaas immana mala ba tamaaretas immides. Iza tamaareti kaththa gaseththishen muleka wuronta ixides! Asay ubbay miidi kalides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye iza tamaareti attida kaththa shisho oykida; izikka tamanee nam7u leemate kumeththi gidides! Kaththay ubbay shiqiday ichashshu soollofenne nam7u moleppe.
