1 Ubbaa miish doommiday Xoossaa gididayssa hayssafe kaallidi nu be7ana. Xoossi alamenne izi bolla diza miish ubbaa usuppun gallassan medhdhides. Xoossi sa7a medhdhidaappe guye sa7ay dhumanne aykkoyka bayndaaro gidadus. Izin buro ay miishshika medhettibeenna. Gido attiin GODAA Ayanay haaththata bolla paallees.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-02.jpg?nolink&}}

1.2 Hessafe guye GODAY, <<Poo7o gido>> gides; poo7o gidides. GODAY poo7ozi lo7o gididayssa be7idi <<Gallas>> gi xeygides. GODAYKKA poo7oza dhumappe shaakkidi omars gi xeygides. GODAY koyro gallas poo7o medhdhides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - Nam7anththo gallas GODAY sa7appe bollan diza saloza medhdhides. GODAY haaththaa salozappe shaakkides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Heedzdzanththo gallas GODAY haaththi mela biittafe shaakettana mala ooththides. GODAY mela biittaa, <<Gade>> haaththaa <<Abba>> gi xeygides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye GODAY, <<Biittay dumma dumma qommo miththatanne atakilteta dolsu>> gides. Izi gida malakka hanides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Oydanththo gallas GODAY arshe, aginanne xoolintteta medhdhides. GODAY istti gallassinne omars, wodetanne layththata shaakkana mala ooththides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Ichchashanththo gallas GODAY haaththa giddon diza kafota ubbaa medhdhides. GODAYKKA hessi lo7o gididayssa be7idi medhettidayta anjjides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Usupunththo gallas GODAY, <<Dumma dumma medosati ubbay biitta bolla gidetto>> gides. GODAY gida malakka hanides. Isttafe baggay meheta, baggati tiran gooshettizayta, baggati do7ata. GODAYKKA hessi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY, <<Ase nu medhanne nu leemiso mala ooththi medhdhoos. Isttaskka biitta bolla ubbaninne medosata bolla maatay do>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessa gishshas GODAY gadeppe biitta ekkidi as medhdhi de7o shemppo izas immides. Iza sunththika Addaame geetettides. GODAY Addaamey ba dizason atakilteta tokkidi dichchana mala heen woththides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY atakilteta giddon nam7u dumma dumma miththata dolisdes. De7o miththinne iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza miththati deettes. GODAY Addaame atakilteta giddon diza iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza erateththa miththaa attoriin atatkilteta giddon diza ay miththafekka maanaas dandayzayssa yootides. Gido attiin iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza miththayfe miikko izi hayqqanayssa yooti woththides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY asi barka dizayssi lo7o gidontayssa yootides. Medosatappe awayssika Addaames maado gidana dandaybeetenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Addaame bolla wolqqama dhiskko yeddides; izi dhiskidaashe iza millaafe issi meqeth ekkidi macca histti ooththidi Addaamekko ehides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Addaamey izo beydi, <<Hanna tana misatawus; iza addefe beettida gishshas macca geetettu. Hessa gishshas asi ba aawanne ba aayo aggidi ba machcheyra issi asho gidana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY addenne macca ba medhan medhdhides. GODAYKKA isttas, <<Corite, naytinne nayta nayti inttes de7etto; biittaka kumite>> gi anjjides. GODAY ooththida miishshi keehi lo7o gididayssa be7ides; ubbaa miishshankka ufayettides. Ubbaykka usuppun gallassan hanides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY laapunththo gallassan medheta ubbaa medhdhi makkides. Hessa gishshas Xoossi ba ooththiza ooso ubbaafe shempides; laapunththa gallassaa anjjidesinne dummasides. Gaasoykka he gallassan ba ooththiza ooso ubbaafe shempides. GODAY salonne sa7a izi giddon diza medhetata medhdhida wode hayssaththo hanides.

**2 Nagaray alame gelides**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 1. Addaameynne iza machcha GODAY isttas tokkida atakilte giddon de7iza wode keehi ufayettishe deettes. Nam7ayka  kallo deettes; gido attiin banta kalloteththan issoy issaas yeellatibeetenna; gaasoyka he wode nagaray alame bolla deenna. Istti atakilteza giddon simerettishe GODAARA haasayettishe deettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Gido attiin genanchcha shooshshay atakilte giddon dees. Izi maccassayo, <<GODAY inttena tumukka atakilte giddon diza miththa ayfeppe awayssafekka monta mala azazidee?>> gi oychchides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3. Maccassayakka, <<GODAY iitanne lo7o erisiza erateththa miththa ayfey attoriin ubba miththa ayfekka nu maanaas dandayizayssa yootides. Gido attiin he miththaa ayfe intte miikko woykko intte bochchiko intte hayqana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 4. Shooshshazi maccassays, <<Hayssi tumu gidenna; intte hayqeketa: intte ayfezappe miza wode intte ayfey doyetanayssa, iitanne kiya eron intte Xoossa mala gidanayssa GODAY eriza gish inttena mopite giday hessassa>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 5. Maccassaya miththaa ayfezi meeththas lo7o, ayfes ufayssizazinne erateth demmanaas yaarechchizaz gididayssa be7ida wode ayfezappe ekka madus. Banara diza ba azinaska immadus; izikka miththaa ayfeza mides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 6. Heerakka nam7aa ayfetikka doyettida. Nam7ayka banti kallo dizayssa akeekidi banta kallo kammanaas boncco may7o mala sikkida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 7.Addezinne maccassaya Xoossa cengursi atakilte giddon simerettishin siydi nam7ayka Xoossa sinththafe baqatida (qotettida). Xoossi Addaame xeygidi, <<Awan de7ay? >> gi oychchides. Addaamey, <<Neni atakilteson simerettishin siyada kallo diza gishshas babbada qotetadis>> gi zaarides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 8. GODAYKKA, <<Ne kallo dizayssa oone nees yootiday? Ta nena, <Mooppa> gida miththay ayfeppe madii?>> gides. Addamikka, <<Hanna ne tanara daana mala immida maccassaya miththaa ayfezappe taas immin madis>> gides. GODAY Maccassayo, <<Hayssi ne ooththidayssi aazee?>> gides. Izakka, <<Tana shooshshi baleththiin madis>> gadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg?nolink&}}

2.9. GODAY shooshshaa, <<Neni qangetidayssa; neni tiran gooshshetana; neni biitta maana; neninne maccasiya intte giddon morke gidana; ne naytinne izi naytikka banta giddon morke gidana; maccassay zarey nees hu7e qoxxana; nenikka izas qirphe madunththana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 10. GODAY maccassayo, <<Miixa wode ta nees ceece darssana; ne sheney ne azinakko gidana; izikka ne bolla godatana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 11. GODAY Addaames, <<Ne machchey qaala siyada ta nena mooppa gida miththay ayfeppe ne mida gish biittay neeppe dendidayssan qangetadus. Ne de7o layth ubbaan ne maana kath un7etta ooththada izippe demmana. Hessafe guye ne biitta gidida gish hayqqada biittan simmana>> gides. Addami ba machcho iza shempora dizayta ubbaa aayo gidida gish <<Heewano>> gi sunththides; izas birshshechay, <<Shemppo immizaaro>> guussa. GODAY mehe galba giigissidi Addaamenne Heewano mayzides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?nolink&}}

  - GODAY, <<Asi  lo7onne iita shaakki eranaas ha7i nuuppe issaa gidides; qasseka zaari ba kushe yeddidi de7o miththa ayfe ekki moonta malanne merinaas de7onta mala>> giidi Addaamenne Heewano lo7iza atakilte giddofe kessides. Hessafe guye de7o miththaykko efiza oge naagana malanne oonikka he de7o miththay ayfe ekki moonta mala wolqama kiitanchcha atakilteson woththides.
