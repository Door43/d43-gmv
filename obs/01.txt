

**1. Medheteth**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Ubbaa miish doommiday Xoossaa gididayssa hayssafe kaallidi nu be7ana. Xoossi alamenne izi bolla diza miish ubbaa usuppun gallassan medhdhides. Xoossi sa7a medhdhidaappe guye sa7ay dhumanne aykkoyka bayndaaro gidadus. Izin buro ay miishshika medhettibeenna. Gido attiin GODAA Ayanay haaththata bolla paallees.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye GODAY, <<Poo7o gido>> gides; poo7o gidides. GODAY poo7ozi lo7o gididayssa be7idi <<Gallas>> gi xeygides. GODAYKKA poo7oza dhumappe shaakkidi omars gi xeygides. GODAY koyro gallas poo7o medhdhides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Nam7anththo gallas GODAY sa7appe bollan diza saloza medhdhides. GODAY haaththaa salozappe shaakkides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Heedzdzanththo gallas GODAY haaththi mela biittafe shaakettana mala ooththides. GODAY mela biittaa, <<Gade>> haaththaa <<Abba>> gi xeygides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye GODAY, <<Biittay dumma dumma qommo miththatanne atakilteta dolsu>> gides. Izi gida malakka hanides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Oydanththo gallas GODAY arshe, aginanne xoolintteta medhdhides. GODAY istti gallassinne omars, wodetanne layththata shaakkana mala ooththides. GODAYKKA ba medhidayssi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Ichchashanththo gallas GODAY haaththa giddon diza kafota ubbaa medhdhides. GODAYKKA hessi lo7o gididayssa be7idi medhettidayta anjjides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Usupunththo gallas GODAY, <<Dumma dumma medosati ubbay biitta bolla gidetto>> gides. GODAY gida malakka hanides. Isttafe baggay meheta, baggati tiran gooshettizayta, baggati do7ata. GODAYKKA hessi lo7o gididayssa be7ides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY, <<Ase nu medhanne nu leemiso mala ooththi medhdhoos. Isttaskka biitta bolla ubbaninne medosata bolla maatay do>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessa gishshas GODAY gadeppe biitta ekkidi as medhdhi de7o shemppo izas immides. Iza sunththika Addaame geetettides. GODAY Addaamey ba dizason atakilteta tokkidi dichchana mala heen woththides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY atakilteta giddon nam7u dumma dumma miththata dolisdes. De7o miththinne iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza miththati deettes. GODAY Addaame atakilteta giddon diza iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza erateththa miththaa attoriin atatkilteta giddon diza ay miththafekka maanaas dandayzayssa yootides. Gido attiin iitanne lo7o shaakki eranaas maaddiza miththayfe miikko izi hayqqanayssa yooti woththides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY asi barka dizayssi lo7o gidontayssa yootides. Medosatappe awayssika Addaames maado gidana dandaybeetenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Addaame bolla wolqqama dhiskko yeddides; izi dhiskidaashe iza millaafe issi meqeth ekkidi macca histti ooththidi Addaamekko ehides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Addaamey izo beydi, <<Hanna tana misatawus; iza addefe beettida gishshas macca geetettu. Hessa gishshas asi ba aawanne ba aayo aggidi ba machcheyra issi asho gidana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY addenne macca ba medhan medhdhides. GODAYKKA isttas, <<Corite, naytinne nayta nayti inttes de7etto; biittaka kumite>> gi anjjides. GODAY ooththida miishshi keehi lo7o gididayssa be7ides; ubbaa miishshankka ufayettides. Ubbaykka usuppun gallassan hanides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY laapunththo gallassan medheta ubbaa medhdhi makkides. Hessa gishshas Xoossi ba ooththiza ooso ubbaafe shempides; laapunththa gallassaa anjjidesinne dummasides. Gaasoykka he gallassan ba ooththiza ooso ubbaafe shempides. GODAY salonne sa7a izi giddon diza medhetata medhdhida wode hayssaththo hanides.

**2 Nagaray alame gelides**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 1. Addaameynne iza machcha GODAY isttas tokkida atakilte giddon de7iza wode keehi ufayettishe deettes. Nam7ayka kallo deettes; gido attiin banta kalloteththan issoy issaas yeellatibeetenna; gaasoyka he wode nagaray alame bolla deenna. Istti atakilteza giddon simerettishe GODAARA haasayettishe deettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 2. Gido attiin genanchcha shooshshay atakilte giddon dees. Izi maccassayo, <<GODAY inttena tumukka atakilte giddon diza miththa ayfeppe awayssafekka monta mala azazidee?>> gi oychchides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg?nolink&}}

  - 3. Maccassayakka, <<GODAY iitanne lo7o erisiza erateththa miththa ayfey attoriin ubba miththa ayfekka nu maanaas dandayizayssa yootides. Gido attiin he miththaa ayfe intte miikko woykko intte bochchiko intte hayqana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Shooshshazi maccassays, <<Hayssi tumu gidenna; intte hayqeketa: intte ayfezappe miza wode intte ayfey doyetanayssa, iitanne kiya eron intte Xoossa mala gidanayssa GODAY eriza gish inttena mopite giday hessassa>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Maccassaya miththaa ayfezi meeththas lo7o, ayfes ufayssizazinne erateth demmanaas yaarechchizaz gididayssa be7ida wode ayfezappe ekka madus. Banara diza ba azinaska immadus; izikka miththaa ayfeza mides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Heerakka nam7aa ayfetikka doyettida. Nam7ayka banti kallo dizayssa akeekidi banta kallo kammanaas boncco may7o mala sikkida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Addezinne maccassaya Xoossa cengursi atakilte giddon simerettishin siydi nam7ayka Xoossa sinththafe baqatida (qotettida). Xoossi Addaame xeygidi, <<Awan de7ay? >> gi oychchides. Addaamey, <<Neni atakilteson simerettishin siyada kallo diza gishshas babbada qotetadis>> gi zaarides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAYKKA, <<Ne kallo dizayssa oone nees yootiday? Ta nena, <Mooppa> gida miththay ayfeppe madii?>> gides. Addamikka, <<Hanna ne tanara daana mala immida maccassaya miththaa ayfezappe taas immin madis>> gides. GODAY Maccassayo, <<Hayssi ne ooththidayssi aazee?>> gides. Izakka, <<Tana shooshshi baleththiin madis>> gadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY shooshshaa, <<Neni qangetidayssa; neni tiran gooshshetana; neni biitta maana; neninne maccasiya intte giddon morke gidana; ne naytinne izi naytikka banta giddon morke gidana; maccassay zarey nees hu7e qoxxana; nenikka izas qirphe madunththana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY maccassayo, <<Miixa wode ta nees ceece darssana; ne sheney ne azinakko gidana; izikka ne bolla godatana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Addaames, <<Ne machchey qaala siyada ta nena mooppa gida miththay ayfeppe ne mida gish biittay neeppe dendidayssan qangetadus. Ne de7o layth ubbaan ne maana kath un7etta ooththada izippe demmana. Hessafe guye ne biitta gidida gish hayqqada biittan simmana>> gides. Addami ba machcho iza shempora dizayta ubbaa aayo gidida gish <<Heewano>> gi sunththides; izas birshshechay, <<Shemppo immizaaro>> guussa. GODAY mehe galba giigissidi Addaamenne Heewano mayzides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY, <<Asi lo7onne iita shaakki eranaas ha7i nuuppe issaa gidides; qasseka zaari ba kushe yeddidi de7o miththa ayfe ekki moonta malanne merinaas de7onta mala>> giidi Addaamenne Heewano lo7iza atakilte giddofe kessides. Hessafe guye de7o miththaykko efiza oge naagana malanne oonikka he de7o miththay ayfe ekki moonta mala wolqama kiitanchcha atakilteson woththides.

**3. Bashsha haath**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Daro wodeppe guye alame bolla daro derey duussi doommides. Isttikka keehi iitatanne wolqqamata gidida. Iitateththi keehi dari dari yida gish GODAY alame ubbaa bashsha haaththan dhayssana gishe qachchides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Gido attiin Nohey GODAA matan teemateth demmides. Nohey ba de7iza layththan diza asaappe bollara geeshshanne mooroppe haakkida asa. GODAY ba yeddana bashsha haaththa gishshas izas yootides. Nohey gita markabe ooththana mala GODAY izas yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAYKKA Nohey markabe ooththana mala yootishe, <<Markabeys adussateththi 140 metire, aahoteththi 23 metire, dhoqqateththi 13.5 metire gido>> gides. Nohey ooththida markabeya miththafe oosetadus; izis heedzdzu fooqey, daro kifleti, kaaraynne maskootey deettes. Markabeyakka Nohenne izaso asaa, biitta bolla diza ubba qommo medoosata bashsha haaththafe naagana malas yootides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Nohey GODAAS azazettidi izi yootida mala Noheynne heedzdzu naa izayti markabeyo ooththida. Markabeya keehi gita gidida gish ooththishin daro layth ekkadus. Nohey yaana bashsha haaththa gishshas deraa minththi seerishe istti GODAAKO simmana mala yootides; gidikkoka istti izi gizayssa ammanibeetenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Nohenne izaso asaa bantasinne medoosatas gidana likke kath dagassana mala azazides. Ubbaa miishshi giigidaappe guye markabeyin gelana wodey gakkidayssa Nohes, iza machcheys, iza naytasinne nayta machchetas yootides. Osppun asati markabeyin gelida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY bashsha haaththafe naagettana mala ubbatappe maccanne adde histtidi medoosatanne kafota Nohekko yeddides. Yarshshos go7iza laappun addenne laappun macca ubba qommo medoosatappe Nohe markabekko yeddides. Ubbaykka markabe giddo gelida mala GODAY barka markabeza penge gordides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye iray buko dommides. Bukkishe bukkishe bukkishe aggonta 40 gallasinne omars kumeth bukkides. Biittafe pulttoy pude pultees; kumeththa alame bolla diza miishshi ubbay haaththan kamettides; gita zumatikka attonta haaththan kamettida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Markabeza giddon diza asatappenne medosatappe attoorin biitta bolla de7iza ubbay hayqqides. Markabeyaka haaththaa bolla pude dendada ba giddon diza shemppota ubbaa hayqoppe ashshadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Irazi bukkizayssa aggidaappe guye markabeya ichchash agina likke haaththa bolla dendadus. Hessafe guye haaththazi pacci pacci bides; gallassatappe issi gallas markabeya zumata xeeran shempadus. Gido attiin alamey hanno gakkanaas haaththan kametta uttadus. Heedzdzu aginappe guyen zumata xeeray beeto doommides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg?nolink&}}

Nohey oyddu tammu gallassafe guye biitta bollafe haaththazi melidaakko eranaas quuro yeddides. Quurozi haanne heenne piradhishe mela biitta koydi demmanaas dandayibeenna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessafe guye Nohey haraphphe yeddides. Iza gidikko ba uttanaso demmanaas dandyonta gish simmada Nohekko yadus. Saamintafe guye Nohey zaaridi haraphpheyo yeddides. Izakka wogara miththa boncco ba doonan ekkada simmadus. Haaththay pacci pacci biza gish doozatikka dolo doommida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Nohey hara issi saaminta naagidaappe guye haraphpheyo heedzdzanththo yuusho yeddides. He wode ba shempana soho demmida gish simmada yabeeku. Haaththaayka meli meli bees.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg?nolink&}}

Nam7u aginappe guye GODAY Nohe, <<Neni, neso asatinne medoosati ubbay markabey giddofe kezite>> gides. Izaso asay markabey giddofe kezida. Daro naytanne naa nayta yelidi biitta kumida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg?nolink&}}

Nohey markabeyppe ke7idaappe guye yarshshoso ooththidi ubba medoosatappe yarshshos shiiqanaas bessizayta GODAAS yarshsho shiishshides. GODAY yarshshozan ufayettidi Nohenne izaso asaa anjjides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg?nolink&}}GODAY, <<Asi ooththiza iita miishsha gish zaarada tani biitta mulekka doqqonta mala qaala gelays. Aykkoska asa nayti yelagateththafe doommidi nagaranchchata gidikkoka alameza ta zaarada bashsha haaththan dhayssike>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg?nolink&}}

Caaqo qaalas malata gidana mala GODAY koyro zuulla ooththides. Ubba wode zuullazi salo bolla beettees. GODAY ha zuulla baggara deraas gelida qaala qoppees.

**4. GODAA caaqo qaala Abrhaamera;**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Bashsha haaththafe haa simmiin daro layththafe guye derey alame bolla zaari tirpides. Ubbayka issi qaalan haasayeettes. GODAY istta azazida mala biitta kumanayssa aggidi issi bolla shiiqettidi katama keexxida.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Isttikka otoranchchatanne Xoossi isttas yootidayssa naagettontayta. Ubbarakka salo gakkanaas aduussa gimbe gimbeth doommida. GODAY hayti iita ooththizayssanne hessan gujji gujji bizayssa be7ides. Hessafekka keehi iita nagara ooththanaas dandayeettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessa gishshas GODAY istta qaala daro dumma dumma qaalan laammidi asaa biitta bolla laallides. Istti ooththanaas doommida katamay sunththi Babiloone geetettawus. Izis sunththa birshshechay bul7akettidaaz woykko hadirdaaz guussa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Xeetu layththafe guyen GODAY Abraame geetettiza bitanezas haasaydes. GODAY iza, <<Ne derenne neso asaa aggada ta nena bessiza biitta ba. Heen ta nena anjjana; bayra dere kessana; ne sunththaa gitasana; nena anjjizayta ta anjjana; nena doqqizayta tani doqqana; ne gaason biitta bolla diza as ubbay anjjetana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Abraamey GODAAS azazettides; ba machcho Soora, ba ashkara ubbatanne baas diza aqota ubbaa ekkidi Xoossi iza bessida Kanaane biitta bides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Abraami Kanaane gakkida wode GODAY, <<Ne yuushon dizayssa ubbaa xeella; ne be7iza biitta ubbaa ta nessinne ne zareththatas laata histta immana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Gallassatappe issi gallas Abraami Melke-Xedeqe geetettiza gita Xoossa qeesetas halaqaara gayttides. Melke-Xedeqey Abraame anjjishe, <<Salaanne sa7a Goday gita Xoossay Abraame anjjo>> gides. Abraami baas diza ubbaafe Melke-Xedeqes asraata immides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?nolink&}}

Abraameynne Sooray daro layththafe guyenka nay baynda deettes. GODAY Abraames zaaridi hidota qaala immides; izas nay daanayssanne iza zarey salo xoolinte mala daranayssa yootides. Abraami GODAY immida hidota qaalan ammanides. Abraami GODAY immida hidota qaalan ammanida gishshas geesh gidi qoodettides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Abraamera caaqo qaala gelides. Caaqo qaalay nam7u bagga asata giddon diza sigeteththa yo7o. GODAYKKA Abraames, <<Ta nees ne gulbateppe naa immana; ne zaretaska Kanaane biitta immana>> gides. He wode Abraames nay deenna.

**5. Hidota immetida naaza**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-01.jpg?nolink&}}

5.1. Tammu layththafe guye Abraaminne Sooray Kanaane gakkida. Hessa gakkanaas nay isttas deenna. Hessa gish Abraame machcha Sooray, <<GODAY taas nayti donta mala diggida gishshasnne takka cimida gishshas ta ashkarayo Aggaaro beya ekka; iza taas naa yelu>> gadus.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Hessa gish Abraami Aggaaro ekkides. Aggaara atuma naa yeladus. Abraami, <<Isma7eele>> gishe izas sunth kessides. Gido attiin Sooray Aggaari bolla qanaatadus; Isma7eeles tammanne heedzdzu layth gidishin,

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-03.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY Abraame, <<Tani ubbaa dandayza Xoossa; nenara tani qaala gelana>> gides. Abraamey liiphe bolla sinththa guth gufannides; GODAYKKA Abraame, <<Neni daro deres aawa gidana. Kanaane biitta neessinne ne zaretas xinxo histta immana. Tanikka merinaas intes Xoossaa gidana; nenikka neson de7iza addeta ubbaa qaxxara>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?nolink&}}

GODAY, <<Ne machcha Sooray attuma naa yelana; izikka hidota immetida naaza gidana; iza sunththaa ne Yisaaqa ga xeygana; ta caaqo qaala izara woththana; izikka bayra dere gidana. Isma7eelekka gita dere histtana; gido attiin ta caaqo qaalay Yisaaqara gidana>> gides. Xoossi Abraame sunththa, <<Abrahaame>> gi laammides; izas birshshechay, <<Darota aawaa>> guussa. Xoossi Soora sunththaka <<Saara>> gi laammides; izas birshshechay <<Darota aayo>> guussa.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Yesusaninne izi inttes ooththida ooson intte ammanikko, intte kirsitaaneta! Xoossi inttena xala7eppenne dhuma kawoteththafe kessidi Xoossa po7o kawoteththan woththides. Xoossi yo7ota intte ooththiza gal7anne nagara ogeza diggidi ooraththinne xillo ooso oge inttes immides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-16.jpg?nolink&}}

Intte kirsitaaneta. Yesusi ooththida ooso gaason Xoossi intte nagara atto gides. Ha7i Xoossi morketeththa qoodhe mata laggeta histti qoodides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-17.jpg?nolink&}}

Xoossa laggenne Yesusa oosanchchata intte gidikko Yesusi tamarsiza miishshatas azazetandeta. Kirsitaaneta intte gidikkoka ha7ikka nagara ooththanaas paacetadeta. Gido attiin Xoossi ammanetidaade gidida gishshas intte nagara paaxizaa gidikko ta atto gaana gees. Intte nagara eqettana wolqay immetana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-18.jpg?nolink&}}

Xoossi intte woossana mala, qaala xana7ana mala, hara kirsitaanetara goynnana mala, izi inttes ooththidayssa haratas yootana mala koyees. Ha yo7oti ubbay izara mino gaytoteththi daana mala istta maaddana.

**50. Yesusi Simmana**

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-01.jpg?nolink&}}

2,000 layththa lagge alame yuushon diza daro asay Yesusa gishshas yootiza mishiraachcho qaala siyshe gam7ides. Woosa keeththi diccashe de7adus. Yesusi wode wurseththan simmi yaanaas hidota qaala immides. Izi buron simmontaakkoka hidotaa qaala naagees.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-02.jpg?nolink&}}

Nuni Yesusa simmo naagishe Xoossa geeshshateththaninne iza boonchchisiza ogen de7ana mala koyees. Qasseka iza kawoteththaa gishshas haratas nu yootana mala koyees. Yesusi biitta bolla de7iza wode, <<Iza kaalliza tamaareti Xoossa kawoteththaa gishshas yootiza mishiraachcho qaala alame bolla ubbason de7iza asaas sabakana. He wode alame wurseththi yaana>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-03.jpg?nolink&}}

Daro kawoteththatan diza dereti ha7ikka Yesusa gishshas siybeetenna. Yesusi pude salo simmanaappe sinththan mishiraachcho qaala siyontaytas awjjana mala kirsitaanetas, <<Biite Kawoteththatan diza deraa tana kaalliza tamaareta histtite!>> gi yootides. Qasseka, <<Kath shiisho wodey gakkides!>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-04.jpg?nolink&}}

Qasseka Yesusi, <<Oosanchchay ba godaappe darenna. Hayssa ha alame daannati tana ixxida mala ta gaason inttena waayssananne wodhana. Intte ha alamezan meto ekkikokka dazgite; tani ha alameza haariza xala7e xoonadis. Intte tanan ammanidi wurseththi gakkanaas minniko he wode Xoossi inttena ashshana!>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-05.jpg?nolink&}}

Alame wurseththi gakkiza wode asa bolla gakkana mish qoncisanaas Yesusi bana kaalliza tamaaretas issi taarike yootides. Izi isttas, <<Issi asi ba goshshan lo7o zereth zerides. Izi ichchida wode morkey yiidi haruma maata zeridi bides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-06.jpg?nolink&}}

Zerettida gistey caarida wode addeza oosanchchati, <Godawu neni hayssa ha goshshan lo7o zereth zerabeekii? Histtiin iza giddon haruma maatay waani dizee?> gi oychchida. Istta godaykka, <Morkey zeronta aggenna> gi zaarides>> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-07.jpg?nolink&}}

<<Oosanchchati banta godaas, <Harumaa shoddinoo?> gi oychchida. Istta goday, <Akkay. Intte hessa ooththiko gistezakka shoddana. Kaththa shiisho wodey gakkanaas takkidi harumaa shiishshidi xuuggite; gisteza gidikko gootaran dagaste> gides.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-09.jpg?nolink&}}

Harumazi iita bagga gidida asata bessees. Haruma zeriday morke Dabiloosa bessees. Kaththa shiisho wodezi alame wurseth bessees. Buucizaytikka Xoossa kiitanchchata besseettes.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-11.jpg?nolink&}}

Qasseka Yesusi alame wurseththafe sinththan biittan simmanayssa yootides. Izi bida mala simmidi yaana; hessika izas asateththa bollay daana. Salo shaaran yaana. Yesusi simmiza wode hayqqida ammaniza asati hayqooppe dendidi salon gayttana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-12.jpg?nolink&}}
